# London Kensington 배경

## BACKGROUND STYLE

Wall: Clean white/off-white stucco, soft blur
Floor: Cream Portland stone pavement
- Floor texture visible but not distracting
- High-end London residential feel

## MOOD

Refined British, timeless simplicity, elegant

## LIGHTING ADAPTATION

Wall and floor MUST reflect the SAME lighting as person:
- If person has warm light: wall shows warm ivory tones
- If person has cool light: wall shows cool pure white tones
- Floor shadows match person's shadow direction exactly
