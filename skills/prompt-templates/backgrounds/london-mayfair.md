# London Mayfair 배경

## BACKGROUND STYLE

Wall: Simple cream/beige stucco, soft blur
Floor: Grey York stone pavement
- Floor texture visible but not distracting
- Classic London residential feel

## MOOD

Elegant British, quiet luxury, refined

## LIGHTING ADAPTATION

Wall and floor MUST reflect the SAME lighting as person:
- If person has warm light: wall shows warm cream tones
- If person has cool light: wall shows cool grey-beige tones
- Floor shadows match person's shadow direction exactly
