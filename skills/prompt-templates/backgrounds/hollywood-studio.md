# Hollywood Outdoor 배경

## BACKGROUND STYLE

Location: Hollywood Hills / Beverly Hills outdoor
- Clean modern architecture, white/cream walls
- Palm trees visible but not distracting (soft blur)
- California sunshine, blue sky hints
Floor: Light concrete, stone pavement, or manicured lawn edge
- Upscale LA residential feel

## MOOD

Luxury California lifestyle, celebrity vibe, aspirational, sun-drenched glamour

## DO

- Use outdoor California lighting (bright, natural)
- Show hints of blue sky or palm trees (blurred)
- Keep modern minimalist architecture feel
- Maintain premium luxury atmosphere

## DON'T

- NO indoor studio look
- NO grey backdrops or gradients
- NO artificial studio lighting
- NO busy street scenes
- NO tourists or crowds

## LIGHTING ADAPTATION

Outdoor background MUST reflect the SAME lighting as person:
- If person has warm light: architecture shows golden hour warmth
- If person has cool light: architecture shows clean bright daylight
- If person has COOL BLUE tone: KEEP the cool blue feeling in background
- Floor shadows match person's shadow direction exactly
