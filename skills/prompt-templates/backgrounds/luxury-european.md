# Luxury European 배경

## BACKGROUND STYLE

Wall: Elegant European architecture, Parisian style
- Cream/beige stone buildings
- Classic ornate details, soft blur
- Haussmann-style facade optional
Floor: Cobblestone or cream limestone pavement
- Classic European residential feel

## MOOD

Luxurious, sophisticated, timeless elegance, refined

## DO

- Use warm cream and beige tones
- Keep architectural details subtle and elegant
- Maintain soft, diffused quality
- Suggest wealth and heritage

## DON'T

- NO modern glass buildings
- NO harsh industrial elements
- NO busy tourist crowds
- NO overly ornate or distracting details

## LIGHTING ADAPTATION

Wall and floor MUST reflect the SAME lighting as person:
- If person has warm light: stone shows golden warm cream
- If person has cool light: stone shows cool ivory/grey-beige
- Floor shadows match person's shadow direction exactly
