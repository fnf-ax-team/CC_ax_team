# London Chelsea 배경

## BACKGROUND STYLE

Wall: Weathered red brick, soft blur, muted terracotta tone
Floor: Dark grey London flagstone
- Floor texture visible but not distracting
- Classic British urban feel

## MOOD

Classic British, understated cool, urban sophisticated

## LIGHTING ADAPTATION

Wall and floor MUST reflect the SAME lighting as person:
- If person has warm light: brick shows warm amber undertones
- If person has cool light: brick shows muted grey-red tones
- Floor shadows match person's shadow direction exactly
